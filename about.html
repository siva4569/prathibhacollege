About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and commitment to education

Welcome to Prathibha Junior And Degree College

Prathibha Junior And Degree College was established in 2007 with a vision to provide quality education and empower students to achieve their dreams. Located in the historic town of Draksharamam, East Godavari, Andhra Pradesh, our institution has been serving the educational needs of the community for over 15 years.

We are committed to providing comprehensive education through our Junior College, Vocational, and Degree programs. Our institution has expanded to three strategic locations - Draksharamam, K Gannavaram, and Kakinada - ensuring accessibility to quality education for students across the region.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal growth and professional success. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence and character development.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and commitment to student success. We aim to produce well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society and excel in their chosen fields.
