About Our College

Committed to providing quality education and shaping future leaders

Empowering Minds Since 2007

Prathibha Junior And Degree College is a premier educational institution located in Draksharamam, East Godavari, Andhra Pradesh. We offer a comprehensive range of programs including Junior College courses, Vocational training, Degree programs, and Distance Education options.

With three branches across Draksharamam, K Gannavaram, and Kakinada, we are committed to making quality education accessible to students from all backgrounds.
